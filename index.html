🍽️ FOOD & DINING

Meal Plan

Breakfast is included daily at the hotel. Lunches and dinners are on your own, except when covered during excursions.

Recommendations

Happy Seafood

Recommended local seafood spot. Fresh catches at reasonable prices — a must-do.

Issara Kata Beach Special Occasion

Upscale beachfront dining overlooking Kata Beach. Stunning sunset views, elevated Thai and international cuisine, great cocktail selection. Arrive 30–45 minutes before sunset. Perfect for a romantic dinner — consider a reservation during peak periods.

Local Tips

  • Beef dishes are generally excellent.
  • Chicken dishes are often less impressive than South African standards.
  • Restaurants directly on the beachfront charge a premium — venues one or two streets back are better value.
  • 7-Eleven is your best friend: great coffee, affordable snacks, toiletries, basic OTC medication and cheap water.

Alcohol Sales

Alcohol is generally sold between 5:00 PM and Midnight at 7-Eleven (subject to local regulations and store policy).
